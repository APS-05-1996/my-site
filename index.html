ESCAPE ARTIST

Escape Artist is a multilingual-rock outfit based in the city of Lucknow. The members derive their musical fundamentals from independently grown artists across India. It has been a constant effort of the band to summarise music as best form of artistry.

Additional Infromation

    Born and brought up in Lucknow; the city of Adab, Tarab and Tehzeeb,
    5 childhood friends and musicians got together and later evolved 
    into a 5 piece Alt/Post Rock Band, which is now called as "Escape Artist"
    Escape Artist happens to be the first Independent Band from Lucknow with 
    all original released music. To their audience, their music is known for 
    their bilingual discography, a unique soundscape, 'not much talked about'
    themes and gripping lyrics. Listening to Escape Artist can be described 
    by every listener in a complete different manner because of the numerous 
    perspectives in their sound and words, but mostly their Music is a journey
    for everyone, and Music is a journey to the band.
